Robot Systems Design Project (Leo Rover)

An ongoing project. This project simulates an engineering project, from design requirements analysis to hardware and software assembly and design. I am currently involved in assembly and software operation in the group.

WorkflowLLM Project

This 2024 project aims to build a database based on the huge workflow files in Apple's shortcuts software to facilitate the training of LLM construction workflows. In the project, I was mainly responsible for the collection and organization of source files, as well as statistical decoding. I also assisted in the data generation process.

Multi-Robot Control Models Research Project

This project in 2023 aims to use machine learning as the general direction. After two weeks of online teaching and reports led by professors, the student groups independently select topics and conduct research. Our group chose to investigate multi-robot control models. I am the team leader in the group. In addition to my own tasks, I am also responsible for arranging the division of labor and following up the progress of team members.

Federated Learning Research Project

This project in 2023 includes a one-week offline research training on IoT technology, as well as subsequent independent research and paper writing. In this project, I chose to study federated learning and conducted a comprehensive analysis of the performance of several federated learning algorithms.
